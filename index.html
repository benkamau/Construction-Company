About Us

“Amateurs talk strategy professionals talk LOGISTICS”

Vermont Consult is a logistics and general engineering company located in Nairobi at Landmark Plaza. Established in 2015, we are focused on the construction industry enabling the movement of construction material from factories and quarries to the construction sites. The company through its various partners has access to a fleet of Tippers, rollers, dozers, water browsers etc. that we lease to our customers as required.


Safety awareness plays a vital role in our daily operations. Our controllers take time to brief our drivers on safety procedures before any engagements.

Our Vision
